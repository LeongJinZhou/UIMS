# QIU University Management Platform

## What This Is

A fully integrated university management platform for QIU that centralises academic planning, timetabling, examination management, HR, and finance into one interconnected system. The platform automates the operational duties of the Head of Programme and Programme Coordinator roles with AI assistance, subject to human approval before any change is published.

## Core Value

Centralize all university operations into a single AI-powered platform that eliminates silos, reduces manual workload, and ensures MQA compliance while maintaining human oversight for critical decisions.

## Context

QIU currently operates with disconnected systems including the existing QIU Connect app and eQIU portal. Academic planning is largely manual, timetabling creates conflicts, and administrative duties consume significant time from academic leaders. The platform must integrate with existing student information systems while providing a modern, unified interface.
